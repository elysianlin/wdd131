Overview

Purpose

This website is a crochet yarn flower booking platform that allows users to explore different types of flowers and their meanings, and make reservations for custom handmade yarn flowers. It combines education and service to create a personalized gifting experience.

Audience

The target audience includes students, young adults, and anyone interested in meaningful gifts, handmade crafts, or flower symbolism. It is especially suitable for people who want to give unique and personalized presents.

Dynamic elements

The webiste will includ seeral dynamic elements using JavaScript:

  • A booking form that allows user to select flower type, color, date, and quality.
  • a flower meaning filter (user click catagry like love or friendship to display matching flowers)
  • a shopping cart or reservation summary that updates in real times.
  • image glory that changes when user click different flowers.

Content

Home page

Welcome to W0re than Flowers, a cozy space where handmade yarn flowers bring warmth and meaning to every moment. Our flowers are carefully crafted to last forever, making them perfect for gifts and special occasions.

Explore different flowers and their meanings, and create your own customized bouquet. Whether it is for love, friendship, or celebration, we help you express your feelings in a unique way.

Flowers

Users can browse different types of yarn flowers and learn their meanings:

  • Rose – Love and passion
  • Tulip – Happiness and warmth
  • Sunflower – Positivity and loyalty
  • Lily – Purity and elegance
